Our Mission

One day, the world will drown in a tide of crimson, and I will ascend to my throne as the Emperor of Sauce!

At Barry B's, our mission is to change the world, one bottle of sauce at a time. We believe in fair compensation and good benefits for our employees. Our founder, Jacob B, is tired of living in a world where greedy corporate leaders--many of whom he deems to be "trust fund babbys"--take a lion's share of the rewards for performing a tiny fraction of the work. Barry B's is an organization for the employees and by the employees, and that will always be a part of our culture. No manager at Barry B's will ever make more than 3 times that of the lowest paid employee in the company.
